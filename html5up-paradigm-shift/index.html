Candidate Statement

My name is Karla Lozada and I am running to be your next Director of the Engineering and Computer Science Department. I have spent my college years developing my leadership and technical skills. As a student in the ECS Department, I understand the struggles students go through such as enrollment, full class sections and lack of sections for students. I also want to work closely with clubs and student organizations to make sure ASI provides all the resources they need to complete their events and goals for the year. I am a Computer Science major but I want to work closely with students with other majors and understand and be aware of their problems. We are going through a difficult time worldwide, but it is still important to think about the person that will represent YOU in front of the ECS Dean and ASI Directors. Also, I understand these couple weeks have been difficult because we transitioned completely online but also each student's personal struggles. I want to represent each one of us and make sure faculty and the ECS Department know and support us through this difficult time. Also, a part of the role of Director of ECS is hosting events for our college and promoting communication and networking opportunities. I want to be able to help students build not only technical skills but also find interesting opportunities to showcase their skills.
