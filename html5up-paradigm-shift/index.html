Candidate Statement

My name is Karla Lozada and I am running to be the next Director of the Engineering and Computer Science Department. I have spent my college years developing my leadership and technical skills, and as a student in the ECS Department I understand many of the struggles students go through. Enrollment is difficult, classes are often full, and there's a lack of availability for core classes. I also want to work closely with clubs and student organizations to make sure ASI provides all the resources they need to complete their events and goals for the year. As Director of ECS, I will cordinate events for our college and promote networking opportunities. I want to help students build not only technical skills but also find interesting opportunities to showcase their talent. We are going through a difficult time worldwide, and I want to be your voice to the ECS Dean and ASI Directors as we navigate these challenges.
